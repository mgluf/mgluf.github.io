About CJG Designs

I have been designing things since my middle school drafting and shop classes in 1974 when I designed my first home - an octagon house, of course. I’ve been drawing ever since. In January of 2003 I started CJG Designs after spending 19 years working in many different areas of the Architectural field. But there is something about helping people with their home that I find more rewarding than any other kind of design work.

One important concept I want to convey to you as you look through this site is that I don’t have a preconceived idea of what your project will look like. I don’t work in one specific style of Architecture. My goal is to turn your vision into reality. I can work from any starting point - a sketch on a napkin, a plan you found in a magazine, or even just a photograph. Your design will be completed to meet your specific needs.
